## Strategies Tested

### 1. Trend Following (EMA 50/200 Crossover) with Leverage
- Enter long on golden cross (EMA50 crosses above EMA200)
- Exit on death cross (EMA50 crosses below EMA200)
- Tested at 1x, 3x, and 5x leverage
- Includes funding cost for leveraged positions

### 2. Momentum Breakout
- Price breaks above 20-day high with 1.5x volume average
- Enter long, trailing stop at 10%
- Tested at 2x leverage

### 3. Grid Trading
- 20 grid levels, 2% spacing
- Buy low levels, sell when price hits next level up
- Capital split across all grid levels

### 4. RSI Mean Reversion with Leverage
- RSI(14) < 30 = oversold, enter long
- Exit on RSI > 50 or 5% stop loss
- Tested at 3x leverage

### 5. Combined Multi-Strategy
- Equal-weight blend of Trend 3x + Momentum 2x + RSI MR 3x
- Tests whether diversification improves risk-adjusted returns
